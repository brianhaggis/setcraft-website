Privacy Policy

Draft · last updated June 2026

Draft for layout. Final Privacy Policy text is provided or approved by Brian. The photo-import disclosure below is the one passage that has to be present and accurate, so it is written out in full.

The short version

Setcraft is a local-first macOS app. Your song library, shows, setlists, lyrics, and chord charts are stored on your Mac. We don't have copies of them, and using the app requires no account.

Setlist photo import

Setlist photo import is the one feature that sends data off your device. When you import a setlist photo, the photo is sent to our server, which forwards it to Anthropic's API to read the setlist text. Only the extracted text comes back to the app, which then matches it against the library already on your Mac.

Your song library, lyrics, and charts are not part of that request and never leave your Mac. Photo import is a paid, online feature; if you never use it, no song data leaves your device through Setcraft.

What we do not collect

  • No copies of your library, setlists, lyrics, or charts.
  • No account is required to download and use the free version.
  • No advertising trackers.

Payment

Purchases run through Lemon Squeezy, our merchant of record. They process payment and issue your license key. We don't see or store your card details.

Contact

Questions about privacy: hello@getsetcraft.com.
